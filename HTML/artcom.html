commissions

art is my hobby, and i do commissions for some income and practice.

im willing to experiment and try new things.

commissions aren't a job to me, so please don't treat them as such.

pricing and timeframe may vary depending on desired the piece.

if you would like art from me, you agree to everything written below.

obligatory TOS stuff

my art cant be used in any ai dataset or resold.

my art should be credited when used and my signature should not be removed.

you need to disclose if my art will be used for commercially and i retain rights to my work.

my work isnt to be used for any hateful or bigoted cause.

pricing is very fluid, and comes down to the difficulty and interest of the piece.

i'd prefer to just, talk about vision and decide from there.

communication is handled through discord, my tag is listed on the homepage.

now, enough of the formal stuff.

i am open to draw just about anything, though i reserve the right to deny any request.

i have more experience with furry art, and thus am better with anthro characters.

any gnc or queer characters are a plus, and im open to have fun with a design.

my timeline isn't concrete, and the maximum time from the begginning of a commission would be around a month.

i can accept several edits to sketches or wip stages for free.

although edits to the final piece may require payment for major changes unless i made a mistake.

i'd like to keep open communication and send updates whenever requested.

contact me through discord to speak further, my discord tag is listed on my homepage.
